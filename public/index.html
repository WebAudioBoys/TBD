Are ya not seeing any MIDI outputs?

If you're on a Mac computer you'll have to open up your MIDI studio!

You'll start by clicking on the magnifying glass on the upper left corner of your screen. Search for and select "Audio MIDI Setup".

Once you've made it there, make sure you're looking at the MIDI studio. If you're not, my dear friend, click on Window and show MIDI studio. If you're some kind of hot shot, you can also hit 'command+2'.

Ok, you should see several MIDI devices in the MIDI studio. Double click on the one that says IAC Driver.

You should see the IAC Driver Properties window pop up! Wow! You can add ports by clicking the plus sign in the lower left quadrant of the window. We here at TBD Enterprises think it'd be best if you added a minimum of threee ports. Name them if you want. That's the fun part.

Thanks for visiting the site and have a good night, okay?
